Welcome to GWDZ

Hello there!

I am a philosophy student. This site is my philosophy journal, as much a consequence of my time spent studying philosophy as a cause of it. Here I post topical explainers, argumentative articles, and shower thoughts on interesting questions.

My main interests are in social epistemology. I want to answer one big question: "how should groups of people form their beliefs?". This question is important because many groups of people aren't good at forming beliefs.

Outside of philosophy, I like cats and fencing (but not fencing cats).

Have fun exploring!
